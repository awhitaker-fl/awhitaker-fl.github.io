{% include step.html n="1" title="Gersh the generator" img="/assets/images/bo1/ascension/ascension-step-1-gersh-device-throw.png" %}
Throw a Gersh Device near the generator I have annotated, located down the stairs from the landing near PHD. If the generator is sucked into the Gersh, the step is complete.

{% include step.html n="2" title="Interact with the terminal" img="/assets/images/bo1/ascension/ascension-step-2-terminal.png" %}
Hold Square on this terminal in the corner of the area after opening the door after Stamin-Up. You will see an Illuminati symbol.
